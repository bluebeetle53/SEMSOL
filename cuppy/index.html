About Cuppy

Florence Otedola

Florence Ifeoluwa Otedola, known professionally as DJ Cuppy, is a Nigerian disc jockey, producer, singer, and songwriter. Born on November 11, 2000, she is the daughter of Nigerian billionaire Femi Otedola.

Cuppy gained recognition after releasing her debut single "I Love My Country" in 2014. She has since become one of Africa's most prominent DJs, performing at major events across the globe.

In 2020, she released her debut EP "Original Copy" which featured hit singles like "Jollof on the Jet" and "Karma". She is also known for her philanthropic work through the Cuppy Foundation.

DJ Cuppy holds a degree in Business and Economics from King's College London and a Master's degree in Music Business from New York University.
